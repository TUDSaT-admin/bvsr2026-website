Mission

BVSR (Bundesverband studentischer Raumfahrt) is the umbrella organization representing student spaceflight groups across Germany.

We bring together motivated students, ambitious projects, and forward-thinking partners to strengthen the student spaceflight ecosystem at a national level.

BVSR Conference 2026

May 14–17, 2026
From May 14–17, 2026, the BVSR Conference is hosted by TUDSaT in Darmstadt. Over four days, students, researchers, and industry representatives will gather for:
— Workshops on spacecraft design, propulsion, and project management.
— Keynotes and Panels from leaders in academia and industry.
— Student Space Forum where projects are presented and discussed.
— Networking Sessions to foster collaboration between universities and companies.
— Social Events to connect the community in a more informal setting.
