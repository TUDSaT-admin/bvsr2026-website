Mission

BVSR (Bundesverband studentischer Raumfahrt) is an umbrella association founded in 2021 in Germany to facilitate networking and knowledge exchange across space related student groups.

The aim is to improve space-related education and produce excellent STEM-graduates in space related field. While the focus is mainly on german-speaking countries student groups from all over Europe are welcome to contribute.

BVSR Conference 2026

May 14–17, 2026

Hosted by TUDSaT in Darmstadt, the BVSR Conference will run from May 14–17, 2026. Across four days, it will bring together students, researchers, and industry representatives to:

→ Workshops on spacecraft design, propulsion, and much more.
→ Keynotes and Panels from leaders in academia and industry.
→ Student Space Forum where projects are presented and discussed.
→ Networking Sessions to foster collaboration.
→ Social Events to connect the community.
