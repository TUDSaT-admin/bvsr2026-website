Privacy Policy

The protection of your personal data is very important to us. This Privacy Policy informs you about the type, scope, and purpose of the collection and use of personal data on the website of the BVSR Conference 2026, organized by TU Darmstadt Space Technology e.V. (TUDSaT).

1. Data Collection

We only collect and process personal data that you voluntarily provide to us, for example, when contacting us via email or filling out forms (e.g., sponsorship form). Typical data may include your name, email address, company, and message.

2. Use of Data

The personal data provided will be used solely for purposes related to the organization and execution of the BVSR Conference 2026 Conference, including responding to inquiries, coordinating sponsorships, and providing updates.

3. Data Sharing

We do not pass on your personal data to third parties without your explicit consent, unless we are legally obliged to do so.

4. Cookies and Logs

Our website may use cookies and server log files to ensure functionality and security. These do not allow direct conclusions about your personal identity.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request information about the data stored about you, its origin and recipient, as well as the purpose of storage. You also have the right to request correction, blocking, or deletion of your data.

6. Contact

For questions regarding data protection, please contact us at: vorstand@tudsat.space.
