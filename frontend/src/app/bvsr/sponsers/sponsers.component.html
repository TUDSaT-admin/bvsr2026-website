Partnership & support

The BVSR Conference is an annual event that brings together space enthusiasts from all over Germany. Both size and impact of this conference are growing steadily from year to year, which also increases the financial resources required. Therefore, we highly appreciate your interest in helping us organize this conference. Your influence can take various forms, which are explained in more detail below:

  1. Monetary or in-kind donations
  2. Sponsorship (various tiers available)
  3. Additional services

Please also understand that this catalog is not yet final and we reserve the right to make changes. Should this be the case, we will of course consult with you to find a satisfactory solution.

Donations

If you would like to make a monetary or in-kind donation outside of the packages listed below, simply get in touch with us. We are grateful for your help! As a token of our gratitude, we usually list all our supporters on our conference website and publish a social media post containing all supporters. If requested, we will happily issue a donation receipt. You will also be explicitly invited to participate in the forum of academic spaceflight.

Sponsoring

The conference sponsorship packages are divided into three categories. Each package opens up new opportunities for you and your company to participate in the BVSR conference and its potential benefits. We are open to your personal suggestions and requests in order to put together a package that suits you as well as possible.

Tier 1

Friends

Starting at 1 000 €

  • Large logo on the conference website
  • Acknowledgment in the form of a dedicated post on social media
  • VIP seat at the forum of academic spaceflight
Tier 2

Sponsors

Starting at 5 000 €

Popular
  • Large logo on the conference website
  • Acknowledgment in the form of a dedicated post on social media
  • Exclusive access to the BVSR CV pool
  • Job posting on our Job Wall (conference website) included
  • Opportunity to organize a workshop included
  • Approx. 3 m² booth at the forum of academic spaceflight included, including private parking at the venue
  • VIP seating at the forum of academic spaceflight
  • Access to the networking lounge at the forum
  • Acknowledgment during the opening ceremony
  • Significant support for the next “Giant Leap”
Tier 3

Primary sponsors

Starting at 15 000 €

  • Main sponsor logo on the conference website
  • Logo placement at the conference (print media, presentation, lanyards/badges, flyers)
  • Acknowledgment in the form of a dedicated post on social media
  • Exclusive access to the BVSR CV pool
  • Pinned job posting on our Job Wall (conference website) for maximum reach
  • Opportunity to organize a workshop included
  • Approx. 6 m² booth at the forum of academic spaceflight with preferred placement, including private parking at the venue
  • VIP seating at the forum of academic spaceflight
  • Access to the networking lounge at the forum
  • Fifteen-minute speaker slot during the opening ceremony
  • Maximum support for the next “Giant Leap”

Additional services

In addition to donations and standard sponsorship, we offer the following services that allow you to tailor your presence at the conference to your specific needs.

Booths at the forum of academic spaceflight

  • Approx. 3 m² for 1 500 €
  • Approx. 6 m² for 2 500 €; Sponsors only pay the difference of €1 000 to upgrade their booth to 6 m².

Workshop slot — 2 000 €

Organization of a two-hour workshop for approximately 5–20 members of the BVSR on a topic of your choice. We are happy to work with you to develop ideas that will appeal to as many interested individuals as possible.

Job / internship wall — 2 000 €

Get the opportunity to upload job vacancies (both for jobs and internships) directly to the BVSR conference website. This website is mainly used for the registration and the program, which means that your job advertisement will be brought directly to the attention of interested individuals from all over Germany.

Questions or a custom package? We would love to hear from you.
